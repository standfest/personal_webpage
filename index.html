In a nutshell

During the last decade I have established a data-centric AI process for decision making along the real estate industry. I became the world’s leading expert for the document class floor plan, and showcased how to solve a variety of related challenges. Throughout my career I have succeeded in digital transformations multiple times by combining deep method, domain and meta knowledge.

How to get in touch

And I am obviously available as public speaker, for data and AI consulting, and to participate in academic research projects.

Past experiences

Since recently, I was founder and CEO at Archilyse, where for six years we provided an End-to-End process covering data acquisition, homogenisation, verification, enrichment, densification and contextualisation in order to convert images of floor plans into actionable insights and charts for decision makers. Our approach has improved state of the art processes in the served industries by magnitudes, replacing otherwise well established dysfunctional subjective processes. Before that I worked with research groups at ETH Zurich, Future Cities Lab, Graz Technical University and in digitisation at Standfest. If you require additional detail you can email me to ask for the latest version of my resume.

Selected projects

I am constantly working on ideas big and small, but always with one objective in mind: using evidence to improve. You can use some of the results for free.

  • Swiss Dwellings — World's largest dataset of apartment models including aggregated geolocation-based simulation results covering viewshed, natural light, traffic noise, centrality and geometric analysis. Provided under CC-BY
  • Archilyse — The full codebase of my previous startup. Provided under AGPL.
  • Robust Funnel — A method to measure both conversion times and conversion probabilities for large sales funnels, and to use Monte Carlo simulations for revenue cash flow predictions. It is adressing the lack of accuracy of weighted funnel predictions, which are used generically.
  • Introducer — A simple tool collection, to leverage the power of ChatGPT in HR workflows.
  • Neufert 4.0 — A research project in collaboration with the InfAr chair of Bauhaus University Weimar.
  • HouseGAN ft. Swiss Dwellings — A workshop at ICCV23 in collaboration with the TU Delft.
